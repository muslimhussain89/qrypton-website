About Us

Who are we?

Qrypton was founded in 2018. Based in Islamabad – Pakistan. We have a global scope, providing solutions to design, animation and contemporary architectural issues in Pakistan. Our highly collaborative, research-based design method involves clients, stakeholders, and experts from a wide range of fields from early on in the creative process. The results are exemplary, outspoken projects that enable our clients to develop towards a better future.

What we do?

Branding, Architecture, Social Media & Interactive.

Branding

Our team of expert designers provide complete branding solutions. We help brands to be recognized in the industry with our unique and creative designs. Ranging from professional logo designs to stationery items we develop the complete brand identity just the way our client demands us to. Qrypton is proud to claim that most of the biggest local brands are our clients.

Architecture

Qrypton has a well-established architecture department and our team excels in providing architectural and interior designs from scratch and provide complete execution of the architectural projects. Our designs are always up to the mark, neat and minimalistic following the modern approaches. Any design a client wants can be designed and executed by Qrypton Design Solutions.

Social Media

At Qrypton we have a specialized team designated for designing a social media campaign for any brand. In the past Qrypton has been able to deliver awesome social media campaigns to its clients by designing all the social media related designs including posters, banners, stories or any design related to all major social media platforms.

Interactive

UI/UX? Yes! We DO provide complete interactive solutions and Qrypton has an awesome web development department. Our web development team along with our design team work with full coordination to ensure that the needs of the clients are met and the designs provided by the design team are implemented in a pixel perfect way by the development team.

Got a design project? Please free to contact us and we will be more than happy to help you out.
